We can create a custom Microsoft Access database for whatever data you wish to collect. Custom reporting of that data gives you any analysis or listing of the information that you require. The database can also be branded to use you company logo, and others such as ISO or IIP, when reporting data.

We can also integrate the database into a company intranet, where users will enter data through an internal web page. This gives the impression of browsing the internet rather than opening a database. This is a very effective way of entering data in a business climate where everything is web orientated.

Benefits of a company database...

  • Data recorded and reported the way you want.
  • Database can be modified to meet any changes in your work.
  • Single and multi-user applications available.
  • Speed of data processing.
