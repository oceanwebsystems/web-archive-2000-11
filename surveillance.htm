Are you aware of how much of your company's time is lost by workers playing games or surfing the Internet? There is an abundance of statistics on how much time is wasted by employees on non work-related Internet access. Of equal importance, however, is the purpose of their visits online. Which sites are they visiting? What are they getting in their e-mail and from whom? OceanWeb Systems can monitor your network over a given timeframe and detect specified activities. We can provide you with a report detailing the following:

  • Websites visited and their category.
  • Applications opened and time spent within them.
  • A log of every keystroke made by a particular user.


Using these facts, it is possible to take positive action, whether it is invoking an Internet Usage Policy or tightening network security. OceanWeb Systems will explain the raw data generated in the report and can assist you with your chosen course of action.
